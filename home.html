How to Delegate your Cardano to Dana Node Pool

1 - Open a free account with any online exchange, like Coinbase.com
2 - Buy Cardano (ADA) in your exchange account
3 - Download a Cardano wallet like Yoroi or Daedalus
4 - Transfer a small amount of Ada from your exchange wallet to your Cardano wallet, verify it, then perform your main transfer
5 - In your Cardano wallet, go to Delegation List, search for DN and click Delegate — Yoroi example shown
6 - Staking will be applied to next epoch. The reward begin to accrue from 3rd epoch (12 days) after staking.
7 - The deposit remains in your wallet. Your keys, your coins, so you can send or withdraw your ADA at any time.
